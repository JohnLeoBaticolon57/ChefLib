Sapin Sapin

Ingredients:

  • Glutinous rice flour
  • Coconut milk
  • Sugar
  • Ube (purple yam) extract
  • Langka (jackfruit) extract
  • Pandan extract
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • Latik (coconut caramel) for topping

Instructions:

  1. Mix glutinous rice flour, coconut milk, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Divide the mixture into portions and add flavorings and food coloring to each portion.
  3. Layer each portion in a greased pan and steam until set.
  4. Top with latik before serving.

Puto Bumbong

Ingredients:

  • Glutinous rice flour
  • Brown sugar
  • Water
  • Bamboo tubes (bumbong)
  • Grated coconut

Instructions:

  1. Soak glutinous rice in water overnight, then grind.
  2. Mix the ground rice with water to form a dough.
  3. Fill bamboo tubes with the dough and steam until cooked.
  4. Serve with grated coconut and brown sugar.

Biko

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Glutinous Rice
  • 3/4 Brown sugar
  • 3 1/2 cups of Dilluted Coconut Milk
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Fried coconut milk(latik)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the glutinous rice.
  2. Simmer the coconut milk sugar mixture.
  3. Cook until thickened.
  4. Prepare your baking dish.
  5. Transfer to the prepared baking dish.
  6. Serve it in banana leaves.

Suman

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup glutinous rice (soaked overnight)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Banana leaves, cut into squares (for wrapping)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice well. Soak it in water overnight.
  2. Drain the rice. In a pot, combine rice, coconut milk, sugar, and salt.
  3. In a steamer, steam the rice mixture for 45-60 minutes, or until cooked through.
  4. If using banana leaves, soften them with heat. Wrap spoonfuls of cooked rice in banana leaves.
  5. Transfer to the plate.
  6. Serve it cool.

Kutsinta

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Cassava Flour Tapioca Starch
  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 cup Brown Sugar (or to taste)
  • 2 cups Water
  • 2 tsp Lye Water
  • Orange Dye Food Coloring
  • Grated Fresh Coconut

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, Combine tapioca starch, all purpose flour, brown sugar and water. Mix it well.
  2. Preapare a 1tsp of water to dissolve the food coloring and addd to the mixture.
  3. Add the mixture with lye water combine and mix it well.
  4. Prepare a molder and put the mixture in it, preheat your steamer.
  5. Arrange your molder in the steamer and steam it in 18-20 mins.
  6. Unmold and arrange it the plate, Serve it with grated fresh coconut.
