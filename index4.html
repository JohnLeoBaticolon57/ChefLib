Halo-Halo

Ingredients:

  • Shaved ice
  • Evaporated milk
  • Sweetened beans
  • Jellies (nata de coco, kaong, gulaman)
  • Fruits (banana, jackfruit, sweet potato)
  • Leche flan
  • Ube halaya (purple yam)
  • Cornflakes or rice crispies
  • Caramel sauce
  • Ice cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Layer shaved ice, sweetened beans, jellies, fruits, leche flan, and ube halaya in a glass or bowl.
  2. Drizzle with evaporated milk and caramel sauce.
  3. Top with cornflakes or rice crispies.
  4. Add a scoop of ice cream if desired.
  5. Mix well before eating.

Leche Flan

Ingredients:

  • Egg yolks
  • Condensed milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and sugar. Add vanilla extract if desired.
  2. Strain the mixture to remove any lumps.
  3. Pour the mixture into llaneras (caramelized molds).
  4. Steam for about 30-40 minutes or until set.
  5. Let it cool, then refrigerate before serving.
  6. To serve, invert onto a plate so the caramel sauce is on top.

Saging con yelo

Ingredients:

  • Ripe saba banana(peeled)
  • Brown sugar
  • Evaporated milk
  • Shaved Ice
  • Vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, boil the saba combine with brown sugar. Cook until banana is slightly soft.
  2. Rest it cool, Prepare your cup. put your cooked saba.
  3. Put the shave ice on top.
  4. Pour evaporated milk on the top.

Buko Pandan

Ingredients:

  • Buko Strips
  • Condensed milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Sago or tapioca Pearl
  • Gelatin(Pandan Flavor)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine Buko strips, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and Gelatin.
  2. Pour the mixture into mold
  3. refrigerate for about 30-40 minutes.
  4. Add your tapioca pearl on top and pour milk(optional)
  5. Serve it cool.

Turon

Ingredients:

  • Banana Saba(peeled)
  • Brown Sugar
  • Lumpia Wrapper
  • Banana Leaves
  • oil

Instructions:

  1. Cut your banana into half.
  2. In you lumpia wrapper arrange your banana and put sugar, and wrap it.
  3. In a pan preheat it and put oil. Put you wrapped banana in the pan and fry.
  4. Add in the outer part sugar to caramelized.
  5. Let it cool, then serve it a plate.
