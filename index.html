Adobo

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken or pork
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, combine chicken/pork, soy sauce, vinegar, water, garlic, onion, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
  2. Bring to a boil, then simmer over low heat for 45 minutes to 1 hour or until meat is tender.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve with steamed rice.

Sinigang

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork belly
  • 1 liter water
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 100g string beans
  • 2-3 pieces taro, cubed
  • 2-3 green chili peppers
  • 1 pack sinigang mix
  • Salt and fish sauce to taste
  • Water spinach (kangkong) leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, boil water and add pork belly. Simmer for 30 minutes or until tender.
  2. Add onion, tomatoes, and taro. Cook for another 10 minutes.
  3. Add radish, eggplant, and string beans. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the sinigang mix and green chili peppers. Stir well.
  5. Season with salt and fish sauce to taste.
  6. Add water spinach leaves and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Kaldereta

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef, cubed
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup liver spread
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and beef broth. Simmer for 1-2 hours or until beef is tender.
  4. Add potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers. Cook for another 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in liver spread and grated cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Simmer for another 5 minutes and serve hot with rice.

Bicol Express

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork belly, sliced
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 5-6 green chili peppers, sliced
  • 3-4 red chili peppers, sliced
  • 2 tbsp shrimp paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add pork belly and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a boil. Simmer for 30 minutes or until pork is tender.
  4. Add shrimp paste and mix well.
  5. Add green and red chili peppers. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Pour in coconut cream and simmer until sauce thickens.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice.

Sisig

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork belly
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3-4 red chili peppers, chopped
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp calamansi juice (or lime juice)
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mayonnaise (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Grill pork belly until cooked and crispy. Chop into small pieces.
  2. In a pan, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped pork and cook until heated through.
  4. Add soy sauce and calamansi juice. Stir well.
  5. Add chopped chili peppers and mix.
  6. Transfer to a sizzling plate, crack an egg on top, and mix before serving.
  7. Optionally, add a dollop of mayonnaise on top. Serve hot.

Tinola

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 thumb-size ginger, sliced
  • 2 green papayas, sliced
  • 2-3 cups water
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Malunggay (moringa) or spinach leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, sauté garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add fish sauce and water. Bring to a boil and simmer for 30 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  4. Add green papayas and cook for another 5-10 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add malunggay or spinach leaves and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Kare-Kare

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg oxtail or beef tripe
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggplants, sliced
  • 1 banana blossom, sliced
  • 1 bunch string beans, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup toasted ground rice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water or beef broth
  • Shrimp paste (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Boil oxtail or tripe until tender. Set aside.
  2. In a pot, heat oil and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add oxtail or tripe and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add water or beef broth and bring to a boil. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add peanut butter and ground rice. Stir until well combined.
  6. Add eggplants, banana blossom, and string beans. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with shrimp paste and steamed rice.

Menudo

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork, cubed
  • 1 cup pork liver, cubed
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 2 carrots, cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add pork and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add soy sauce and tomato sauce. Stir well.
  4. Add water and bring to a boil. Simmer for 30 minutes or until pork is tender.
  5. Add potatoes and carrots. Cook until tender.
  6. Add pork liver and bell peppers. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.
